Coffee Origins

Some History

  • Coffee: is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffea species.
  • 850 AD Started in Abyssinia, now Ethiopia the original home of the coffee (arabica)
  • 1414 introduced to Mecca, Medina, Yemen, Cairo, Damascus, Constantinopla
  • 1554 Ottoman Empire
  • 1565 Europe
  • 1720 Americas

2018 Coffee Quick Facts

  • The total economic impact of the coffee industry in the United States in 2018 was $225.2 billion
  • Coffee-related economic activity comprises approximately 1.6% of the total U.S. gross domestic product
  • Consumers spent $74.2 billion on coffee in 2018
  • The coffee industry is responsible for 1,694,710 jobs in the US economy
  • The coffee industry generates nearly $28 billion in taxes (including ancillary goods)
  • 2nd Most Popular drink after water
  • 1600 million cups a day
  • 250 cups sold per day per store
